About Me

My name is Aubrie Stromberg and I'm a Senior at Texas A&M University where I'm studying Visualization, specializing in Interactive Media and Graphic Design.

Creating has always been a passion of mine. I love expressing myself through art, photography, and design. People and music are my biggest inspirations because they offer a whole new view of the world, their uniqueness is something I try to capture through abstraction and color. I hope my art would inspire people to seek beauty and joy in their surroundings.
